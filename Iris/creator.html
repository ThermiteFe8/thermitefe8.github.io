About Jamie Bautista

Jamie Bautista graduated cum laude from the Ateneo de Manila University with a degree in Communications and has a Masters in Entrepreneurship from the Asian Center for Entrepreneurship and Asian Institute of Management. He taught classes in English literature and comcis theory and appreciation at Ateneo de Manila University, The Beacon School, Assumption College, and Xavier School. He is currently also general manager at LSA Printing Press and publisher of Nautilus Comics. He has pubilshed the National Book Award winning Siglo: Freedom and Sigol: Passion. He is the creator of the long-running comic series Cast, which is his other collaboration with Private Iris co-creator Arnold Arre.
