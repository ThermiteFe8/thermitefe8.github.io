Case 12: The Inner Circle

Credits:

  • Story by Jamie Bautista
  • Pencils/Inks by Jim Jimenez
  • Colors by Jamie Bautista

Release Date: August 28th, 2010

Youtube Version: Real Gone from Cars

Description:

What is the secret of MacGuffin Plains? And how does it tie into the things Iris Able has been investigating such as her mother's whereabouts, the origin of her headquarters, and the mysterious Holding Company? Iris is about to embark on her most dangerous case yet as she discovers the story of the Inner Circle. But will she still be able to crack the case now that she no longer has the help of her former friend, Danton Cord? Written by Jamie Bautista (Cast, Siglo: Passion), with art by Jim, Jay, Joy Jimenez JBrothers.
